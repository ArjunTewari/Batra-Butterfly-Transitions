Exclusive Advantages

Features Only Batra Butterfly Has

01

AI Photo Stock Management

Point your phone camera at a box or product, and the AI automatically reads the article code and suggests a stock update. Confirm with one tap. BUSY has no image processing capability whatsoever.

Unique to Batra Butterfly
02

Face-Scan Attendance

Staff clock in daily using face recognition — no punching cards, no proxies. The system matches the live camera feed against enrolled face descriptors in real time and logs attendance automatically.

Unique to Batra Butterfly
03

Native Mobile App

A full-featured companion app for Android and iOS built with Expo/React Native. Staff can create invoices, scan stock, mark attendance, and view their performance — all from their phone in the field.

Unique to Batra Butterfly
04

Payment Clearance Approval Workflow

Any staff member can submit a payment clearance request. It sits in a "Pending" queue and only deducts from the retailer's ledger after the master explicitly approves it — preventing accidental or fraudulent entries.

Unique to Batra Butterfly
05

Retailer Under-Buying Alerts

The analytics engine monitors each retailer's purchase history and flags those whose recent buying has dropped below their historical average — giving you actionable data to reach out before losing a client.

Unique to Batra Butterfly
06

Challan-Format PDF Estimates

Invoices export as PDF estimates in the exact format your physical challans use — Challan No, M/S, date, item rows, grand total. Staff can view online or download a file they send directly to the retailer.

Unique to Batra Butterfly
07

Staff Loans & HR Overview

Track advance loans per staff member, compute net payable salary (earnings minus deductions minus active loans), and record payment history — all in a single staff detail page, without needing a separate payroll module.

Unique to Batra Butterfly
08

Real-Time Retailer Growth Tracking

Month-over-month and year-over-year purchase growth rates computed per retailer. The top-retailer dashboard ranks customers by sales volume, purchase frequency, and average order value.

Unique to Batra Butterfly
